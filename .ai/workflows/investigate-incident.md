# Workflow: Investigate Incident

## Discovery
- Capture incident symptoms, scope, and timeline.

## Impact Graph
- Identify impacted modules, users, and data paths.

## Risk Analysis
- Assess ongoing risk and containment urgency.

## Compatibility Verification
- Validate mitigation does not introduce new breakage.

## Rollback Path
- Prefer immediate safe rollback when uncertainty is high.

## Docs Updates
- Update postmortem references and affected runbooks.

## Test Expansion
- Add regression checks reproducing the incident.

## Telemetry Validation
- Ensure root-cause signals are observable.

## Post-Change Governance Updates
- Add new checklist/rule to prevent recurrence.
